Electrified Locks, Exit Devices, Panic Hardware, Magnetic Locks and More

Access control systems are a type of security product that regulates who or what can enter a facility or areas within a facility.

Electrified hardware uses power to control the locking and unlocking of a door. In recent years it has increased in popularity throughout hospitals, schools, government buildings and offices, especially in specific areas such as stairwells doors, and fire safety exits.

We offer a wide range of electrified hardware products, including:

  • Low Energy Automatic Door Operators
  • Delayed Egress Devices
  • Electrified Cylindrical Locks
  • Electrified Exit Devices
  • Electrified Mortise Locks
  • Electrified Strikes
  • Key and Exit Switches
  • Magnetic Locks
  • Motion Sensors and No Touch Switches
  • Panic Hardware Options
  • Push Plates, Actuators, and Touch Panels
