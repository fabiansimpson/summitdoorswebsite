About us

Our story

Summit Windows & Doors, Inc., also known as Summit Doors & Hardware, was established in 1990 in Anchorage, Alaska. In 2015, the company expanded, opening an office in Bryan, TX to serve Bryan–College Station and surrounding markets. We are highly regarded by general contractors for our collaborative approach, maintaining strong relationships through active involvement in project design, budgeting, and implementation. This hands-on approach helps ensure efficiency, accuracy, and successful project outcomes.

Summit Doors' core values

Summit’s goal is to provide competitive and complete bids and to successfully complete projects, all while maintaining the highest level of customer service. Our team values integrity, accountability, professionalism, and maintaining excellence in all we do.
