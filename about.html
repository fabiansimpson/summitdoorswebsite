Our story

Stewart Barnes stated the company in 1987. He has strong relationships with the GC’s in the Anchorage Market in Alaska. Aarika, Stewarts daughter is the General Manager and Project Manager here in the Texas Market.

Our Primary market is College Station area, however we work with Vaughn on a number of HHS and MDACC projects in Houston. We have strong relationships with our General Contractors and are recognized as highly involved in the projects. We see it as our responsibility to work on mitigating any Risk you as the GC may encounter, by identifying risk related to Doors, Frames and Hardware before they are encountered. We work hard to get you the best value and a great long-term solution.
